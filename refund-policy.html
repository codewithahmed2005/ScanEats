Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 2026
📌 Quick Summary: We offer a 14-days free trial so you can test ScanEats before committing. Once you purchase a subscription, all payments are non-refundable. You can choose not to renew at the end of your subscription period.

1. Free Trial Policy

At ScanEats, we believe in complete transparency. That's why we offer a 14-days full-feature free trial to all new users. During this trial period, you can:

  • Create and manage your digital menu
  • Generate QR codes for your restaurant
  • Test all features of the platform
  • Evaluate if ScanEats is the right solution for your business

No credit card is required to start your free trial. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends without any charges.

2. No Refund Policy

All subscription payments are final and non-refundable. Once a plan (3, 6, or 12 months) is purchased and activated, we do not provide refunds for any reason, including:

  • Change of mind or business decision
  • Not using the service enough
  • Technical issues outside our control
  • Business closure

We encourage all users to fully utilize the 14-days free trial before making a purchase decision. This ensures you are completely satisfied with the service before committing.

3. Cancellation Policy

No automatic recurring charges are applied. Your subscription will expire at the end of the purchased plan duration (3, 6, or 12 months). You can choose to:

  • Renew: Purchase a new plan before expiry to continue uninterrupted service.
  • Cancel: Simply do not renew upon expiry. Your account will revert to read-only mode or be downgraded.

To cancel your subscription, please contact our support team before your current plan expires.

4. Exceptional Circumstances (Double Payments)

Refunds will only be evaluated in the following exceptional case:

  • Duplicate Transaction: If you are charged twice for the same subscription due to a payment gateway technical error or system glitch.

In such cases, please contact us immediately at support@scaneats.com with your payment receipt and transaction details. We will verify the duplicate transaction and process a refund for the extra payment within 5-7 business days.

5. How to Request a Refund (if eligible)

If you believe you qualify for a refund under the exceptional circumstances described above, please:

  • Email us at support@scaneats.com
  • Include your registered email address, order ID, and payment receipt
  • Provide a brief explanation of the issue

We will respond within 2 business days and process the refund if your case is valid.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Refund Policy, please reach out to us:

Email: support@scaneats.com
