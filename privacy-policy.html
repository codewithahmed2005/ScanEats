Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At ScanEats, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital menu and QR code generation platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Restaurant name, owner name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Menu Data: All menu items, descriptions, prices, categories, and images you upload to your digital menu.
  • Usage Analytics: Non-personal data such as QR scan counts, menu views, and customer interactions (no personal customer data is collected).
  • Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed securely through third-party payment gateways. We do not store credit card or UPI details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To host and display your digital menu to customers via QR codes.
  • To generate and manage QR codes for your restaurant.
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account status.
  • To send service updates, billing notifications, and important account information.
  • To improve our platform through analytics and performance monitoring.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with unauthorized third parties. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for payment processing (Razorpay) and hosting. These providers have their own privacy policies and security measures in place. We do not share your data with them beyond what is necessary for service delivery.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account data at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
